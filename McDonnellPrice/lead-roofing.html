Lead Roofing Work

Expert Lead Roofing

Lead has stood the test of time as a very durable building material, providing roofers with great flexibility for installing roof flashings and coverings. It is the most recoverable and recycled building material in use today, and will outlast any synthetically produced alternative — making it better value for money.

We offer a wide range of rolled lead sheet adaptations, ranging from simple roof flashings at valleys, abutments and roof penetrations to more complex detail works including bay and dormer windows, spires, cornices, parapets and box gutters.

Our Lead Work Services

  • Expert lead roofing maintenance services
  • Patch repairs to all lead roofs
  • Replacement work and new installations
  • Lead sheets, cladding and flashings
  • All maintenance and care work undertaken
  • Full guarantee on all lead roofing work

Trained Specialists

All work is carried out to good practice guidelines by our own roofers who are trained in the correct installation techniques, as well as our specialist lead installers. Our ability to carry out all associated lead work involved within roof construction eliminates the need to employ another party, making the programming of other trades much easier and providing peace of mind through using one contractor.

Why Choose Lead?

  • Outstanding longevity — outlasts all synthetic alternatives
  • Fully recyclable and environmentally responsible
  • Highly flexible — suitable for complex roof details
  • Proven track record across centuries of use
  • Better long-term value than alternative flashings
