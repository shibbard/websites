Flat Roofing

Cold Liquid Applied Systems

We offer a range of liquid applied waterproofing systems for roof coating with long-term protection for simple, detailed or complicated roof structures.

The MARISEAL SYSTEM is the technologically advanced, premium liquid polyurethane waterproofing system that offers easy application, full certifications, and most importantly guaranteed long-lasting results. It can be used as a new flat roof coating, but also for repair or renewal.

Single Ply Membrane

Single Ply is available in many forms and applications, from commercial roofs to residential roofs, either as a new application or refurbishment. These methods can be loose laid, mechanically fixed or fully adhered, all depending on the build-up of the roof.

10, 15 and 20-year guarantees are available. Electronic and heat suppression tests are also available to confirm that the membrane has been installed correctly.

Hot Melt Systems

This material is mainly used for structural waterproofing which includes car parks and podium decks. The main benefit of using this material is that it can be laid at 0-degree pitch.

Bituminous Felts

These come in a range of applications from single layer to three layers, including a self-healing membrane ideal for terraces and roofs where you have numerous penetrations from plant etc. With all these products you can introduce cut-to-falls insulation or uniform boards.

Green Roofing

Green roofs have enjoyed a huge surge in popularity over recent years due to their wide range of benefits:

  • Reduced rainwater run-off
  • Enhanced roof insulation properties
  • Attractive visual appearance
  • Reduction in urban heat island effect
  • Enhances roof lifespan by protecting underlay waterproofing
  • Encourages biodiversity
