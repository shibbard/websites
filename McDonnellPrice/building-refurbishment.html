Building Refurbishment & Maintenance

Our Building Services

Working throughout Gloucestershire and the South West, we regularly complete small building works for private domestic customers and local authorities. Working to high quality standards and all current building and safety regulations, we assure exceptional quality of workmanship.

Our scope of work ranges from maintenance contracts for private customers and flood wall defence systems for local authorities, to Grade Listed refurbishment projects. We specialise in minor building works consisting of loft insulation to reinstating natural Cotswold dry stone walls, with further works such as general bricklaying and plastering.

For recent refurbishment works, we have been awarded a Civic Award from Cheltenham Borough Council for the restoration of 18 Park Place, Cheltenham.

House Building Services

  • Foundations and preparatory works
  • All general construction and bricklaying
  • New build pitch and flat roof fitting
  • Loft insulation installation
  • Natural Cotswold dry stone wall reinstatement
  • General plastering works
  • Flood wall defence systems
  • Maintenance contracts for private and public sector clients

Quality Assured

All works are carried out in compliance with current building regulations and safety standards. Our team is CSCS compliant and we provide Method Statements and Risk Assessments for every contract.
