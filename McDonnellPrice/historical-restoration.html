Historical Building Restoration

Specialist Heritage Restoration

In recognising the very specialist requirements for restoring and maintaining Listed buildings, we have developed a sector entirely devoted to their successful preservation. Supplying only the most able and qualified skilled craftsmen and finest materials, we take great pride in our ability to redefine these ancient structures by bringing them back as closely as possible to their former glory.

Our expertise does not only extend to historic buildings of the past — many of the skilled restoration techniques and services can also be used to revitalise more contemporary or commercial buildings. We aim as a company to bring former developments back to their original glory and magnificence.

Civic Award — 18 Park Place, Cheltenham

Award-Winning Project

We are proud to have received a Civic Award from Cheltenham Borough Council for the restoration of No. 18 Park Place, Cheltenham. This project demonstrates our commitment to the highest standards of heritage craftsmanship and our ability to deliver complex restoration works to an exceptional standard.

Our Restoration Capabilities

  • Grade Listed building restoration and refurbishment
  • Conservation area roofing and building works
  • Matching of original materials — slate, stone, lead and tile
  • Natural Cotswold stone and dry stone wall reinstatement
  • Traditional lime mortar and masonry pointing
  • Ornamental lead work restoration
  • Historic roof structure repairs and replacement
  • Revitalisation of contemporary and commercial buildings

Why Choose Us for Heritage Work?

Our team of skilled craftsmen have accumulated decades of experience working on historic and listed properties. We understand the importance of sourcing appropriate materials, working within planning and conservation requirements, and delivering results that respect the character and integrity of each building.
