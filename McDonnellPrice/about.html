About McDonnell-Price

Our Story

McDonnell-Price Roofing Contractors Limited has over 60 years experience in the roofing industry. Our team of highly skilled craftsmen endeavour to give the greatest quality and commitment possible to all our clients.

With a workforce of around 30 skilled employees we carry out a wide range of roofing services throughout the South of England. We specialise in providing roofing and waterproofing services from maintenance and repairs, through to re-roofing, new build and larger commercial projects.

Although we specialise in a vast array of roof coverings, we also have a small but developing building sector, which has secured civic awards for the restoration of Grade Listed buildings.

We have established a strong relationship with several major house builders, including Barratt Homes and David Wilson Homes. We also have a strong presence in the private residential sector, offering a competitively priced and professional service from repairs and maintenance through to stripping and re-roofing works. All works are strictly supervised to ensure complete customer satisfaction and comply with NHBC standards.
