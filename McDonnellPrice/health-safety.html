Health & Safety

Our Commitment to Safety

The company operates a fully comprehensive Health and Safety management system within its operations and holds regular Safety Development Group meetings to review performance, set appropriate targets and to agree policy.

Our policy is to have a fully compliant CSCS workforce, which is backed up with both induction training as well as on-going safety awareness initiatives.

McDonnell-Price Roofing Limited is a member of the NFRC and British Safety Council. We also hold safety accreditation with both CHAS and with the NHBC Safe Mark Scheme, as well as being members of Construction Line and Exor.

Regulations

We conform to all applicable Health & Safety regulations. We provide written Method Statements and Risk Assessments for every individual contract.
