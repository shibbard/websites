Service Intelligence FAQ

Common Questions

Answers to what customers ask most often before booking.

What happens if you can’t fix my device?

We operate a strict no fix, no fee policy on all repair and diagnostic work. If we cannot fix your device, you pay nothing. We will always be transparent about what is and isn’t repairable before any work begins.

Do you use genuine parts for Apple repairs?

We use genuine-grade OEM components that meet or exceed original Apple specifications. We are iTechnician certified, and all parts are sourced from verified suppliers. We will always confirm part quality with you before proceeding.

How long does a typical repair take?

Many common repairs — including screen replacements, battery swaps, and virus removal — are completed on the same day, often within a few hours. More complex repairs such as logic board work or data recovery may take 1–3 days. We always give you a clear timeframe upfront.

Will my data be safe during a repair?

We take data security seriously. Where possible, we advise backing up before any repair. We will never wipe or access your personal data without your explicit consent. For repairs that may affect storage, we discuss backup options with you first.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes. All repairs carry a 12-month parts and labour warranty. If the same fault recurs within 12 months of repair, we will fix it at no additional cost.

Can I get a quote before dropping my device in?

Absolutely. Call us on 01453 821410 or email sales@deltanine.co.uk and we’ll give you an indicative price based on your description. A confirmed quote is provided after free in-store diagnostics.
