About JAX Electrical Services

We're not just your local electricians — we are experts in our trade with over 25 years of combined experience.

Our Story

Founded on Experience & Trust

JAX Electrical was founded by Josh Tuck and Max Willsher, who have over 25 years of electrical installation experience between them — all trained to the latest 18th Edition regulations.

The company's personal approach to its customers helps maintain a friendly and lasting relationship. JAX aims to give every customer the very best level of service so they can be assured that projects will be completed to a high standard, on time, and at the right cost.

All work undertaken will be tested and a copy of the certificate sent upon completion. Whether it's a complete rewire, bonding upgrade, distribution board change or just a socket replacement, we're here to help.
