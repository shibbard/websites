Careers & Training

Shape the Future of Electrical Engineering

We champion the cause for trainees. The future of our company — and the industry — relies on the training of the next generation of electricians.

We look to take on apprentices every year, rotating them with our senior electricians to ensure a wide and thorough training base. Every apprentice is reviewed quarterly and their achievements are recognised and rewarded.

We also work with local secondary schools to offer work experience placements, giving students an insight into what it takes to become a qualified electrician.
