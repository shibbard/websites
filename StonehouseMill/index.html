Est. 1922 — Stonehouse, Gloucestershire

A Century of
Integrity

From our founding in 1922, Stonehouse Paper & Bag Mills has remained a cornerstone of British industrial heritage. What began as a local operation supplying the Cotswolds has evolved into a global leader in bespoke paper solutions.

We honour our past by maintaining the same machinery that built our reputation; now augmented by state-of-the-art sustainability practices. Every sheet we produce is a testament to the hands that have guided this mill for over a hundred years.
