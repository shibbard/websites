The Story

Meet Steve
Raines-Gardiner

Hi, I'm Steve — founder and head chef of SRG Catering. I started this company during the first lockdown of 2020, never anticipating how quickly it would grow. Nine months in, we were already going from strength to strength, and we haven't stopped since.

I offer an extensive variety of food, from afternoon tea through to wedding breakfasts, always with the same commitment: restaurant-quality cuisine, wherever you are. Our private dining experiences have quickly become our most sought-after offering.

We strive for excellence and are always working hard to improve everything we do. Every order, every event, every plate — it matters to us deeply.
