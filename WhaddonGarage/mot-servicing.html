The Importance of Your Annual MOT

DVSA Approved Vehicle Testing Station

An MOT is a legal requirement for all vehicles over three years old. It ensures your car meets minimum environmental and road safety standards. At Whaddon Garage, we provide a transparent, objective assessment of your vehicle.

  • Legal ComplianceKeep your vehicle road-legal and validly insured.
  • Safety FirstIdentify potential faults before they become dangerous.
