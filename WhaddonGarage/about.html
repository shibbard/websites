Our Story

Independent, Personal & Fully Guaranteed

We're an independent, privately owned business, and we want our customers to keep coming back because we give genuinely good service — not because they have nowhere else to go.

We have been in this business for over 40 years, and we guarantee all our work for 12 months. Our people have been fully trained and have many years' track record in their jobs. Some staff have completed apprenticeships and have studied at college to NVQ3 level.

Our tyre expert has a 20-year track record working for a major UK tyre supplier, and most of our team has 15–20 years of hands-on experience across a wide range of top makes and models.

Our Capabilities

Specialist Equipment for Almost Any Job

We have the specialist tools and equipment needed for almost any job required on a road vehicle up to 3 tons.

Cam & Timing Belt System

We have a specialist system for cam and timing belt replacement across a wide range of engine types — a critical job that must be done precisely.

MIG Welding

Full MIG welding capability to MOT standard for metalwork repairs. Ideal for structural repairs and chassis work that must pass a test.

Car Electronics & Diagnostics

Our advanced diagnostic machine connects to virtually any vehicle for engine management computer analysis. Free code reads while you wait.

Courtesy Cars & Vans

We have courtesy cars and vans available to keep you mobile while we work. No charge — you only pay for the fuel you use. Must be booked in advance.
