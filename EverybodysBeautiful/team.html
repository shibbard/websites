Our Team

Salon Owner & Experienced Beauty Therapist

Cheryl Hodges

Cheryl has been the proud owner of Everybody's Beautiful since 2009, bringing years of expertise and a passion for beauty and skincare to the salon. With a strong foundation in beauty therapy, she has expanded her skills through a variety of advanced qualifications, including CACI Microdermabrasion, Eve Taylor London Skincare, Spray Tanning, Gelish Gel Nails, Lash Extensions, and more.

Cheryl loves offering a diverse range of treatments, from nails and lashes to facials, ensuring her clients always feel their best. She is deeply passionate about the brands used in the salon and enjoys helping clients discover the perfect skincare routine to suit their individual needs. Dedicated to creating a welcoming and professional experience, Cheryl looks forward to seeing you in the salon.

Experienced Beauty Therapist

Hayley Haines

With 19 years experience in the beauty industry, Hayley brings so much experience and knowledge to Everybody's Beautiful. Hayley first qualified in Beauty Therapy NVQ Levels 2 and 3 and a national diploma in Indian Head Massage in 2006.

Hayley has gone on to work in various 4 and 5 star spas within the Gloucestershire area, working with top skincare ranges including ESPA, Decleor, Jessica, Fake Bake and SpaFind. Hayley joined Everybody's Beautiful in 2018 and has further extended her training with Eve Taylor skincare and SkinBase Microdermabrasion. Her favourite treatments are massage and facials, and she welcomes you with a friendly, professional manner.

Clinic Nurse

Kate McEvoy

Kate McEvoy is a Registered Nurse who qualified in 1995, and an Independent Nurse Prescriber from The University of the West of England (2018). She is currently Lead Practice Nurse at a GP Practice.

Kate has completed her Aesthetics training with Derma Medical in London and is insured with Cosmetic Insure. She currently offers anti-wrinkle injections, excessive sweating injections and dermal fillers using the most up to date techniques. Kate provides a full no-obligation consultation to formulate a personalised treatment plan, aiming to offer subtle solutions to restore a healthy, youthful appearance.

Beauty Therapist & Reiki Master

Micky Parnell

Micky is a skilled Beauty Therapist and Reiki Master with a passion for helping clients look and feel their best. With extensive experience in skincare, wellness, and holistic healing, Micky combines expert beauty treatments with the transformative power of Reiki to promote relaxation, balance, and overall well-being.

Dedicated to personalised care, Micky tailors each session to suit individual needs — whether it's a soothing facial, a rejuvenating massage, or an energy-balancing Reiki session. Micky stays up to date with the latest beauty and wellness trends, and believes in the power of healing touch and positive energy.

Beauty Therapist

Jade Hodges

Meet Jade, who joined our team in January 2025. Qualified in lash lifting and tinting, facials, manicures and pedicures (including gel), Jade is dedicated to refining her techniques to provide the best results.

Massage & Facial Therapist

Hana Fujimoto

Hana is an experienced practitioner and qualified aromatherapist, graduating in 2019 in holistic and deep tissue massage. Her holistic approach means that each treatment is tailored to the client's specific needs, combining techniques such as fascia release and deep tissue.

Hana trained in London and practiced at Neal's Yard Remedies in Bristol for five years. She implements methods of acupressure and Gua sha to promote detoxification and deep relaxation, and has also trained in Eve Taylor Skincare facial techniques. Qualifications include ITEC Level 3 Holistic Massage, ITEC Level 3 Aromatherapy, and CPD in Deep Tissue Massage from BCMB.

Beauty Therapist

Elisha Cave

Elisha is a qualified Level 2 therapist who joined the Everybody's Beautiful team in November 2025. At 18, Elisha is passionate about beauty and wellbeing and loves welcoming clients into a calm, relaxed environment.

She is caring, attentive, and takes real pride in ensuring every client feels comfortable. Elisha is continuing to grow her skills and build her client base — a lovely choice for clients who enjoy a warm personal approach. She looks forward to welcoming both new and returning clients.
