Treatment Descriptions

Express Facial: Refresh the skin with aromatic cleansers, buff away dulling skin cells, and infuse with a nourishing masque. Hydrating moisturisers protect and revive.

Essential Facial: A prescriptive facial tailored to your current skin condition. Deeply cleanse, gently exfoliate, and prepare the skin for aromatic massage oils and luxurious masques. Skin is left replenished, hydrated and protected.

Revitalizing Spring Facial: Customised with the uplifting scent of Rose, this treatment re-energises the face and revives the delicate skin of the neck and décolleté by boosting hydration, toning and nourishing.

Ultra Bright Facial: Illuminate dull, pigmented skin with professional resurfacing, Vitamin C serums and speciality masques. Leaves skin flawlessly radiant and glowing.

Skin Quenching Facial: Restore hydration, plump the skin and soften fine lines. Ideal for parched post-holiday skin or as pre-holiday prep.

Intensive Facial: Intensive exfoliants resurface the skin, combined with potent aromatic blends, targeted serums and customised masques for maximum results.

Cryogenic Facial: The ultimate in skin firming and nourishment. Professional resurfacing and age-defying formulations leave skin smooth, soft and rejuvenated.

Monu Resurface & Peel: A skin peel for mature skin, blocked pores, breakouts, acne, pigmentation, uneven skin tone and texture. Ask us for more information.

To find out more in-depth information on our facial range, please enquire by asking at the salon.
