Before Your Spray Tan

  • ✓ Exfoliate your skin
  • ✓ Do not apply any moisturisers, make up or deodorant
  • ✓ Wear loose fitting clothes to your appointment
  • ✓ Wear flip flops

After Your Spray Tan

  • ✓ Let your tan develop for 8 hours
  • ✓ First shower: water only, no soap (excess tan may wash away)
  • ✓ Pat dry — never rub
  • ✓ Keep skin hydrated with moisturiser
