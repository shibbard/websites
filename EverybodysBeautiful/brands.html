Our Brands

We only use professional, premium brands trusted by therapists worldwide

Eve Taylor London

British skincare at its finest — over 60 years of heritage. Eve Taylor products are developed specifically for professional beauty therapists and are rigorously tested. All products are paraben-free, cruelty-free, and formulated without artificial colours, fragrances, alcohols, formaldehyde, petrochemicals or phthalates. Active aromatherapy ingredients work deeper than the upper skin layer, delivering highly effective, tailored results for every skin type and condition.

Used in our facial treatments and available to purchase in salon.

Gelish Gel Nails & Soft Gel Tips

Gelish is known worldwide for its long-lasting, hard-wearing and instantly dry nail colour. It helps strengthen your natural nail without causing damage — a pH balancing agent stabilises moisture levels and the foundation gel's adhesive properties attract to the keratin in the nail rather than depleting it. Available in a huge range of colours.

SkinBase Microdermabrasion

Voted 'Best Advanced Treatment', SkinBase Microdermabrasion is an effective, intense exfoliating treatment. Ultra-fine crystals buff away the outer layer of old skin cells to reveal fresh, healthy skin beneath. A non-surgical approach to skin rejuvenation — you will never have a dull, lacklustre complexion again.

SkinBase Hydro NEW

A versatile, non-invasive treatment designed to cleanse, exfoliate, purify, hydrate and revitalise the skin with an infusion of powerful ingredients. With a range of serums to choose from, you can achieve healthy, glowing skin from the very first treatment.

Marvellash — Eyelash Extensions

Semi-permanent lash extensions that create longer, fuller and glamorous lashes without the need for curling or mascara. Available in classic, hybrid and volume styles.

tint. Spray Tanning

True tone. True tan. Whether you want a subtle glow or a deep bronzed look, tint. has got you covered. Using the highest quality ingredients and innovative technology, tint. professional spray tan is easy to apply, fast drying and streak free.

Elim MediHeel & MediHand

Elim is a medical grade pedicure and manicure treatment that has transformed the market. Using state-of-the-art ingredients to effectively remove hard skin and calluses from the feet and hands, delivering outstanding results every time. We use both Elim MediHeel and MediHand products in our pedicures and manicures.
