Aesthetics

Medical aesthetic treatments with our Registered Nurse Kate McEvoy

Free no-obligation consultations available. To book, please call us on 01453 823903. Kate will provide a full consultation where you can discuss your areas of concern and formulate a personalised treatment plan together.

About Nurse Kate

Kate McEvoy is a Registered Nurse who qualified in 1995, and an Independent Nurse Prescriber from The University of the West of England. She is currently Lead Practice Nurse at a GP Practice and has completed her Aesthetics training with Derma Medical in London. Kate is insured with Cosmetic Insure.

Kate aims to offer subtle solutions to help restore a healthy, youthful appearance — filling lines and reducing wrinkles using the most up to date techniques.
