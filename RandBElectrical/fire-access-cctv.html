Fire Detection, Access Control & CCTV

Protecting your property, staff and assets with professionally installed fire safety and security systems.

Fire Detection

Automatic fire detection is commonly used to protect property by ensuring the early attendance of the fire service. The need for a fire alarm system is normally determined by the authority responsible for enforcing fire safety legislation and/or a fire risk assessment.

In general, it is appropriate to install some form of fire alarm system in virtually all buildings. Whatever the size, complexity or application of your fire detection system, we have the expertise to help — installing, maintaining and monitoring your system to comply with both Local Authority and insurers' requirements.

Access Control

Access Control Systems are designed and installed to provide protection against unwanted visitors and allow free movement for authorised users where there is frequent personnel traffic.

The correct Access Control solution provides protection to employees, visitors, stock and property. In simple terms, systems provide control of entry or exit through nominated doors via a control panel and electric locking facility — as simple or complex as you require, always providing easy passage for permitted persons.

CCTV

Vast improvements in the CCTV industry have led to improved picture quality and digitally recorded evidence. High-speed, self-managing cameras are now used by clients who need to protect their staff, buildings and profit without interrupting day-to-day business.

Systems can be locally monitored by on-site security staff, or remotely monitored off-site by a specialised monitoring station. We provide full service and installation in domestic and commercial properties, along with offsite monitoring. Visual and audio connections available.
