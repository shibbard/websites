Electrical Installation & Design

The core business of R & B Electrical Installations Ltd is the design and installation of electrical systems within the domestic and commercial sectors, undertaken to the highest standards and latest IEE wiring regulations.

Our service incorporates all aspects of design, installation, testing and inspection — from domestic fault finding to complete re-wiring of private dwellings and retail units. Our highly specialised team can design your project from the initial planning stage through to final completion, whether it's a minor alteration or a complete re-wire.

We also carry out responsive repairs and electrical safety checks for the private and letting agency sector, as well as PAT testing. A periodic inspection report is essential for all landlords and home owners — every electrical installation deteriorates with age and use, so it's vital each installation is serviceable and above all safe.
