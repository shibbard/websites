What We Offer

Our Full Range of Building Services

No job is too big or too small. Here's a full overview of the skills and services we provide across Stroud and Gloucestershire.

Extensions & Refurbishments

Single and double storey extensions, kitchen extensions, loft conversions and full property refurbishments. We handle everything from groundwork to final finishes.

Learn more →

Conservatories & Porches

Bespoke conservatory bases and builds — from traditional hardwood timber to PVCu with FENSA-accredited craftsmen. No ties with specific manufacturers.

Learn more →

Brick / Block / Stonework

Expert brickwork, blockwork and traditional stonework including re-pointing. We work with local Farmington stone and other materials to match your existing property.

Plastering & Rendering

Internal plastering, external rendering and ceiling work. High-quality finishes for new builds and renovation projects alike.

Carpentry

Skilled carpentry work including structural timber framing, oak porches, doors, windows and bespoke joinery. We use trusted carpenters with years of experience.

Guttering & Fascias

Replacement and repair of guttering, fascias and soffits. Including specialist finlock gutter repairs — a common issue in Gloucestershire properties.

Groundwork & Drainage

All aspects of groundwork including foundations, drainage systems, footings and ground preparation for extensions and new builds.

Painting & Decorating

Interior and exterior painting and decorating services to complement any building project, using quality materials and neat, professional finishes.

Patios, Fencing & Landscaping

Garden landscaping, stone and paved patios, boundary fencing and raised beds. We can transform your outdoor space alongside any building project.

Insurance Claims Work

Experienced with insurance claim repairs — we can work directly with insurers and loss adjusters to restore your property to its original condition.

Conversions & Driveways

Garage conversions, barn conversions and new driveways. We handle all aspects of the conversion process from planning to completion.

General Building & Repairs

No job is too small. From minor repairs and maintenance to structural work — we're always happy to help with any building issue across the Stroud area.
