Case Study

Bespoke Conservatory & Landscaping, Cheltenham

Cheltenham, Gloucestershire

What the project involved

  • Lifting the old existing patio area
  • Demolishing an old boundary wall full of shrubs
  • Excavating approximately 40 tonnes of soil
  • New footings for retaining wall and conservatory base
  • Retaining wall in concrete block and Farmington stone
  • Conservatory base in Farmington stone matching the house
  • Bespoke handmade hardwood timber conservatory
  • Stone coins on corners — repeated inside
  • Underfloor heating and tiled floor installed
  • Stone steps to elevated garden area
  • Finished with slabs and stone chippings

"The clients were extremely happy with the work and still keep in contact now."
