Case Study

Bespoke Conservatory & Landscaping, Cheltenham

A recent project showing the full scope of what Ian Rice Building Ltd can deliver — from demolition and groundwork to a stunning bespoke hardwood conservatory.

Cheltenham, Gloucestershire

What the project involved:

  • Lifting the old existing patio area
  • Demolishing an old boundary wall full of old shrubs
  • Excavating approximately 40 tonnes of soil
  • Digging new footings for a retaining wall and conservatory base
  • Retaining wall built in concrete block and Farmington stone to match existing
  • Conservatory base constructed in Farmington stone to match the house
  • Bespoke hardwood timber conservatory — handmade and machined painted
  • Stone coins on corners — repeated inside the conservatory
  • Underfloor heating and tiled floor installed
  • Retaining wall capped with flagstones; stone steps formed to elevated garden
  • Finished with slabs and stone chippings so everything blended in

"The clients were extremely happy with the work and still keep in contact now."
