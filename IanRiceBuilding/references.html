Client References & Reviews

Ian Rice Building Ltd is available to help with a wide range of building projects. Below are genuine comments and references received from our clients — their names and addresses have been supplied, though not all are fully displayed.

Extensions – Stonehouse
"Ian Rice adapted existing accommodation to create a downstairs bedroom and en suite for my disabled mother and then built us a single-storey extension to add a dining area to the lounge and to enlarge the tiny kitchen to form a good-sized, practical one.

It was evident from the start that Ian was a skilled and knowledgeable builder. He kept us informed of what he was doing at each stage and why and he gave good, sound advice when decisions had to be made. The team of specialists he brought in at appropriate stages were well chosen and also produced excellent work.

The results are absolutely stunning and have thoroughly impressed our friends, who have particularly commented on the quality of workmanship throughout. Ian is totally honest, scrupulously fair and unwilling to compromise on the quality of his work. He went out of his way to produce satisfactory solutions to problems and to avoid excessive costs.

I have no hesitation in recommending him to anyone needing a good builder."
Rosemary Jenner Extension, Stonehouse
Conservatories – Stroud
"We use Ian Rice Building's service for the construction of most of our conservatory bases for our own clients. Given that we have our own reputation to uphold, the diverse range of products we undertake and the quality of what we need to ultimately provide, then having a builder we can trust and obtain very high standards and accuracy from is paramount. We have such a builder and are happy to continue work with Ian Rice."
Kevin Poole — Ellwood Windows Conservatories Ltd, Tewkesbury Conservatory Bases, Stroud

Extension and Porch – Stonehouse, Stroud
"Ian built our two storey extension & porch, which incorporates a bedroom, landing, dining room, bathroom, utility room and adapting an existing bedroom and finally a patio. Ian provided an excellent service, very friendly, reliable & considerate to my husband's disabilities and made adaptations such as a ramp for a mobility scooter to access the porch.

Ian was very honest with any challenges that arose, he not only found a solution, but followed it through — we feel he went way beyond the call of duty. Ian provided a complete team of tradesmen, so I did not need to find an electrician or plumber etc.

I would certainly recommend Ian to my friends & family — he made what could be a very stressful situation, enjoyable so we could sit back and watch our home develop. Our two young children miss his early morning visits. Ian was very punctual, he came at the same time every day and my children would wait for him to arrive with his cheerful disposition.

Thank you so much Ian, for not only doing your job, but being the genuine person you are — we are so grateful."
Mr & Mrs Wilkins Extension & Porch, Festival Road, Stonehouse, Gloucestershire
