About Us

Warmth & Nostalgia
in Every Bite

Nestled in the serene surroundings of Ashton Keynes village, Jennie's Kitchen is a charming café that embodies warmth and nostalgia. Guests are greeted by a warm ambiance reminiscent of home, with friendly staff and lovely, inviting décor that enhances the overall vibe.

Our menu features a full breakfast range served until noon, ensuring you can start your day right. Diners rave about the homemade dishes — with standout offerings like the gluten-free fruit crumble and the unforgettable coffee cake, which many proclaim as the best they've ever had.

Whether you choose to sit indoors or bask in the beauty of our flowered garden, the setting is tranquil and welcoming. With reasonable prices and a delightful atmosphere, Jennie's Kitchen is a must-visit.
