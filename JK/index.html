About Us

Warmth & Nostalgia
in Every Bite

Nestled in the beautiful surroundings of the Cotswold Water Park at Ashton Keynes, Jennie's Kitchen is somewhere truly special. A warm welcome, real home cooking and a stunning courtyard garden — this is the café that keeps people coming back.

We serve full breakfasts until noon, hearty homemade lunches, classic afternoon teas and freshly made cakes every day. Everything is cooked to order using quality, seasonal ingredients. Vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free options throughout the menu.

Fully licensed, dog friendly and family friendly — whether you're popping in for a coffee and scone or settling in for a leisurely Sunday lunch, you'll always leave feeling looked after.
