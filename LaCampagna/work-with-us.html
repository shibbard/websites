Work With Us

Join a team where nothing is too much trouble

La Campagna is a wonderful neighbourhood restaurant that offers a vibrant, warm and welcoming dining experience with a unique combination of a traditional pub and an Italian Brasserie.

Our goal is to captivate our guests with the highest quality of food, wine and service through the excellence of our team — who are not afraid to let their personality shine and are at the core of our success.

The pace is fast, standards are high and our guests know that "Nothing is too much trouble" for our team. So we are always looking for energetic, bright and attentive staff with a can-do and will-do attitude, to help them achieve their goals within a highly respected business.

If you think you've got what it takes to be a part of a winning team — or you have the credentials to lead one — we would love to hear from you.

Ready to Apply?

Send your CV along with a covering letter stating the position you are looking for. We look forward to hearing from you.
